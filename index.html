Hello! I am a frontend developer with 10 years experience in financial industry and master's degree in project management. After moving to Stockholm in 2020 I decided to turn my life around and to become a full-time frontend developer. Currently enrolled in a remote frontend bootcamp for 24 weeks (graduating in June 2021) at Technigo in Stockholm and delivering weekly projects covering everything from the basics of programming to structuring web projects in the latest technologies.

My best feature is that I am an ambitious, hardworking and result oriented analytical problemsolver, passionate both about tech and financial industry. I am excited over the endless opportunities that programming skills have opened up to me and I am looking forward to combine my past experience with newly acquired programming skills to create awesome, meaningful products for people to enjoy.
